Welcome to MGL Higher Secondary School

Welcome to MGL School Srinagar Kashmir India. R.P School is situated on the outskirts of Srinagar city. The school is situated at a distance of one kilometer from Kashmir University Naseem Bagh Campus. The famous Dal Lake lies at a negligible distance to the south of the school premises. The school is still in its developing process and in the brief span of time it has got the status of one among the 10 top schools of the Kashmir valley. The school has got three branches.
